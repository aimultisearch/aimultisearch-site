Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about AI Multi Search.

What is AI Multi Search?
⌄

AI Multi Search lets you ask one question and receive answers from multiple AI providers at the same time, so you can compare responses easily.

Which AI providers are available?
⌄

The available AI providers depend on your plan and settings. You can enable or disable providers anytime in Settings → AIs displayed.

What plans are available?
⌄

AI Multi Search offers three plans:

  • Basic (free)
  • Pro
  • Ultra

Available features, AI providers, and usage limits depend on your plan.

Is AI Multi Search free?
⌄

Yes. A Basic (free) plan is available. Pro and Ultra unlock additional features, providers, or higher limits.

How do subscriptions work?
⌄

All subscriptions are managed securely by Google Play. Payments, renewals, and cancellations are handled directly through your Google account.

Can I cancel my subscription?
⌄

Yes. You can cancel anytime via Google Play. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.

What is the Insight function?
⌄

The Insight function helps you turn multiple AI answers into clearer takeaways.

It analyzes responses from different AI providers and generates structured results such as Summary • Analysis • Conclusion • Common Points • Differences • Insight.

Does the app store my questions?
⌄

AI Multi Search only forwards your request to the selected AI providers. Some providers may process requests according to their own privacy policies.

Are my saved results private?
⌄

Yes. Saved results are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared.

Why do different AIs give different answers?
⌄

Each AI provider has its own training data, style, and reasoning methods. Differences are normal and are exactly what AI Multi Search is designed to show.

What are credits?
⌄

Some AI providers may use credits depending on usage.

AI Multi Search allows all users on the Basic (free) plan to try certain premium AI providers every day, so you can explore advanced models before upgrading.

Can I choose how answers are displayed?
⌄

Yes. You can select different result layouts such as vertical list, carousel, or grid.

Change it anytime in Settings → Results layout.

How can I report a bug or send feedback?
⌄

Go to Settings → Support & feedback. Your message will be sent by email with a clear subject for faster handling.

Who is AI Multi Search for?
⌄

Anyone who wants to compare AI answers, double-check information, or explore different explanations and writing styles.
