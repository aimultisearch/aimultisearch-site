Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about AI Multi Search.

What is AI Multi Search?

AI Multi Search is an app that lets you ask one question and receive answers from multiple AI models at the same time, so you can compare responses easily.

Which AI models are used?

The available AI providers depend on your plan and settings. You can enable or disable providers at any time in Settings → AIs displayed.

Is AI Multi Search free?

Yes. A free plan is available. Premium and Premium+ plans unlock additional features, providers, or higher limits.

How do subscriptions work?

All subscriptions are managed securely by Google Play. Payments, renewals, and cancellations are handled directly through your Google account.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel anytime via Google Play. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Does the app store my questions?

No. AI Multi Search does not store your questions on its own servers. Requests are forwarded to the selected AI providers only.

Are my saved results private?

Yes. Saved results are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared.

Why do different AIs give different answers?

Each AI model has its own training data, style, and way of reasoning. Differences are normal and are exactly what AI Multi Search is designed to show.

What are credits?

Some AI providers use credits depending on usage. You can view and manage credits (when applicable) in Settings → AIs displayed.

Can I choose how answers are displayed?

Yes. You can select different result layouts (vertical list, carousel, grid) in Settings → Results layout.

How can I report a bug or send feedback?

Go to Settings → Support & feedback. Your message will be sent by email with a clear subject so it can be handled efficiently.

Who is AI Multi Search for?

Anyone who wants to compare AI answers, double-check information, or explore different explanations and writing styles.
