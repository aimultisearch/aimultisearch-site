How AI Multi Search works

A quick guide to understand how to search, compare and use multiple AIs at once.

What AI Multi Search does

AI Multi Search lets you ask one question and receive answers from multiple AI models at the same time.

This allows you to compare responses, styles and accuracy — all in one place.

How to search

  • Tap the search bar at the top of the screen.
  • Type anything you want to ask, learn or compare.
  • You can also use the microphone to dictate your question.
  • Press the search button to start.

You can stop a search at any time using the stop button.

Understanding results

Each answer is displayed inside its own card, one per AI provider.

  • Copy — copy the answer to your clipboard.
  • Voice — listen to the answer read aloud.
  • Expand — show more of the answer.
  • Expand all — display the full response (depending on your plan).

Providers & plans

Available AI providers and features depend on your subscription plan.

  • Free — limited providers and credits.
  • Premium — more providers and longer answers.
  • Premium+ — full access and maximum limits.

Saving results

  • Tap the save button to store your current search.
  • Saved results are stored locally on your device.
  • You can access them later from the saved results screen.

Privacy in 20 seconds

AI Multi Search acts as an intermediary between you and AI providers.

Your question is sent to the selected providers to generate answers. No personal data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Full details are available in the Terms of Service & Privacy Policy.
