How AI Multi Search works

A quick guide to understand how to search, compare and use multiple AIs at once.

1) What AI Multi Search does

AI Multi Search lets you ask one question and receive answers from multiple AI models at the same time.

This allows you to compare responses, styles and accuracy ‚Äî all in one place.

2) How to search

  • Tap the search bar at the top of the screen.
  • Type anything you want to ask, learn or compare.
  • You can also use the microphone to dictate your question.
  • Tap the search button to start.

3) Understanding results

Each AI answer is displayed in its own card so you can clearly see where the response comes from.

This makes it easy to compare explanations, tone, structure and accuracy.

4) Copy & voice actions

  • Copy: instantly copy an answer to your clipboard.
  • Voice: listen to the answer using text-to-speech.

5) Saving results

  • Tap the save icon to store a result.
  • Saved results are stored locally on your device.
  • You can find them later in the Saved results section.

6) Privacy in 20 seconds

AI Multi Search only forwards your request to the selected AI providers.

Your questions are not stored on external servers by the app itself.

7) Results layout (how to read answers)

You can choose how results are displayed, depending on what feels most comfortable for you.

  • Vertical list: answers stacked one under another.
  • Single card (carousel): swipe between AI answers.
  • Grid of cards: compact view for quick comparison.

You can change the layout anytime in Settings ‚Üí Results layout.
