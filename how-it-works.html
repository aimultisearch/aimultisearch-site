1) What AI Multi Search does
Ask once and receive answers from multiple AI models.
⌄

AI Multi Search lets you ask one question and receive answers from multiple AI models at the same time.

This lets you compare responses, styles, and perspectives — all in one place.

2) How to search
Type or speak your question and launch the search.
⌄
  • Tap the search bar at the top of the screen.
  • Type anything you want to ask, explore, or compare.
  • You can also use the microphone to dictate your question.
  • Tap the search button to start.
3) Understanding results
Each AI answer appears in its own card for easier comparison.
⌄

Each AI answer is displayed in its own card so you can clearly see where the response comes from.

This makes it easy to compare explanations, tone, structure, and different points of view.

4) Insight function
Turn multiple AI answers into clearer takeaways.
⌄

The Insight function helps you turn multiple AI answers into clearer takeaways.

It analyzes responses from different AI models and generates structured results such as Summary • Analysis • Conclusion • Common Points • Differences • Insight.

This makes it easier to understand key ideas, patterns, and disagreements between AI answers.

5) Results layout
Choose the view that feels most comfortable for you.
⌄

You can choose how results are displayed, depending on what feels most comfortable for you.

  • Vertical list: answers stacked one under another.
  • Single card (carousel): swipe between AI answers.
  • Grid of cards: compact view for quick comparison.

You can change the layout anytime in Settings → Results layout.

6) Expanding answers
Preview quickly or open more content when needed.
⌄
  • Expand (🔽): shows more content for one AI.
  • Expand All (⏬): shows the full answer, depending on your plan.

This helps you scan quickly and expand only what really interests you.

7) Useful actions
Copy, listen, and save useful answers.
⌄
  • Copy: instantly copy an answer to your clipboard.
  • Voice: listen to the answer using text-to-speech.
  • Save: store useful results locally on your device.

Saved answers can be found later in the Saved results section.

While voice playback is active, the icon changes to stop so you can stop instantly.

8) Managing providers
Control which AI providers are enabled in the app.
⌄

You are always in control of which AIs are used.

  • Enable or disable providers.
  • See which providers are available for your plan.
  • Manage credits for supported providers.

Your selections are remembered automatically.

9) Privacy
Understand how requests are handled.
⌄

AI Multi Search only forwards your request to the selected AI providers.

Some providers may process requests according to their own privacy policies.

10) Performance
Adjust the app for smoother battery-friendly use.
⌄
  • Battery saver mode reduces background work.
  • Reduced animations simplifies UI effects.

Available in Settings → Performance.

11) Support
Report bugs, ask for help, or suggest improvements.
⌄

Need help or want to improve the app?

  • Report a bug.
  • Suggest an improvement.
  • Ask for help.

Messages are sent by email with a clear subject for easier handling.

12) Tips for better results
Clearer questions usually lead to better comparisons.
⌄

The more precise your question, the better the comparison.

  • “Explain like I’m 10”
  • “Give pros and cons”
  • “Short answer vs detailed answer”
  • “Compare from different perspectives”

AI Multi Search is designed to help you see differences clearly — not just pick a single “best” AI.

Tap any section to expand or collapse it.
